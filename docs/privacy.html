Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

No Data Collection

NetWorth Finance Planner does not collect, transmit, share, or store any personal or financial data on any server. All data you enter into the app — including account balances, income, expenses, and financial goals — is stored exclusively on your device using your device's local storage.

No Internet Required

The app functions entirely offline. No data is ever sent to or received from any external server. We have no access to your financial information.

No Accounts or Registration

NetWorth Finance Planner does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data-sharing services.

Disclaimer

NetWorth Finance Planner is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. Projections and estimates are illustrative only and should not be relied upon for investment or financial decisions. Please consult a qualified financial professional for advice specific to your situation.

Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.
